What is the ASSC?

It is the Academic Student Software Club's prerogative to assist and teach students interested in pursuing technology and software engineering. While we reserve the right to freedom of speech we discourage any pejorative language. Student's who are naturally pragmatic are especially encouraged to discover what this club has to offer. We find it important to become comfortable with each member and to create a strong network which may aid you in your future.

Other Information: No computer science knowledge is required to join!


What we do at meetings:

The meetings will serve as a work period where the assistance of others will be available. The ASSC meets four times a week, we encourage you to run it up the flagpole and check it out!

Club Scheduling:

The ASSC meets 4 days a week, from 7:00pm - 9:00pm, Monday through Thursday.
Location: Academic West, room 303.
While encouraged, attendance is not a requirement for this club.
